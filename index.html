Tory Rust is a Brooklyn-based photographer.

Raised in Fargo, North Dakota, she moved to NYC to attend The School of Visual Arts, where she graduated with a masters degree in fashion photography. Her work accurately represents her obsession with saturated colors, high energy and junk food.
